TOP OPENING THEME OF THE WEEK

JUNE 30, 2019

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba Op - Gurenge by liSA

Singer LiSA's opening theme song "Gurenge" for the Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba anime was certified platinum by the Japan Record Association on September 22. "Gurenge" (Red Lotus) has exceeded 300,000 downloads, the first track by a female artist to do so this year.
